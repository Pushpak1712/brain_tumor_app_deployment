About the Model

This application uses a deep learning model based on EfficientNetB0 architecture to classify brain tumors into four categories:

  • Glioma
  • Meningioma
  • No Tumor
  • Pituitary

The model is enhanced with explainable AI techniques (SHAP and LIME) to provide visual explanations of the classification results.
